Advanced Tech

Elevator Technologies

MR Geared

Dedicated machine room with motor & gearbox. Ideal for heights up to 75m and speeds of 2.5 m/s.

MRL Gearless

No separate machine room (PMSM motor in hoistway). Serves 500m+ heights with speeds up to 10 m/s.

Hydraulic

Fluid pressure powered. Simple and reliable for low-rise buildings (up to 6 floors, 18m).
