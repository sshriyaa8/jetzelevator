Enhanced Safety

01

Advanced Authentication System

We use biometric, numeric and RFID lock to control the elevator. This boosts safety and makes access management easier, ensuring passenger hall access.

02

Multi-beam Light Curtains

The elevator has sensors that use Multiple Infra-Beam Infrared Beams to detect the whole doorway. If a person or object is seen, a series of paper-thin doors will quickly open.

03

Speed Safety Gear

Jetz employs Advanced Safety Gears designed for high speeds, usually above 1.0 m/s, and lifts for speeds up to 2.50 m/s. These gears have built-in stops to control force increases, ensuring a gradual and controlled stop in case of over-speed.

FEATURES THAT REDUCE MAINTENANCE

Advanced Features

01

Advanced Door Control System

JETZ uses synchronous, notable motor and VVVF closed loop control system. This technology enables doors to open and close comfortably with low noise, greater stability and delivers reliable performance. The doors also come with Anti-Squeeze technology.

02

Grease-Less Rails

Our advanced grease-less rails are crafted from self-lubricating materials, eliminating the need for traditional lubricants. This innovation not only removes grease and dust accumulation but also addresses their root cause lubricants.

03

Enhanced Fault Detection And Reporting System

Prevention is key, and with JETZ, we keep you informed of any elevator error. For instance, if your emergency batteries run low or you neglect the servicing, the system takes care, safely stopping the elevator and opening the doors, securing you never get stuck.
